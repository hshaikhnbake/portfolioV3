My Story

In my past life I ran successful real estate practice for over 5+ years using click funnels, landing pages and coding scripts to automate my business processes. My entrepreneurial spirit drove me to build an e-commerce clothing website that brought Pakistani Menswear to a North American audience.

I was obsessed with converting traditional businesses into the digital age. Having a degree in Psychology, I have always been amused by what drives human behaviour and the importance of providing an unparalleled user experience through the means of code. My passion of programming became one that found me and I knew it was my true calling.

Switching my career path was not an easy step, but after enrolling in Juno College's Web Development Bootcamp, it was the best decision I ever made. I am truly grateful for all the opportunites I have been able to achieve and I always share my story as an example of seeing the impossible as possible.
